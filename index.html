Ode to Things: Parker Clay Merkato Signature Tote Bag

Merkato Signature Tote

"An everyday bag that pairs versatility with superior quality. There’s a reason it’s everyone’s favorite. This bag goes where you go."

I received my second Parker Clay bag recently and so far I am in love with the quality, style, and background story that goes into each Parker Clay Piece. Parker Clay was founded to help communities in Ethiopia by providing employment for locals to make quality leather goods. Each piece is handmade and the crafstmanship is evident. I love that when I buy from Parker Clay I am investing in a piece that will last a lifetime and am helping others along the way.

Details

  • Crafted from premium Ethiopian full grain leather
  • Please note that since leather is a natural material, each product can have slight color, texture, and grain variations
  • Premium interior zipper pocket
  • Unlined interior
  • Fits a 15" laptop
  • Interior D-ring to hook keys

Dimensions

  • 14.5" L x 5" W x 13" H
  • 10" handle drop
  • Approximately 2 lbs
